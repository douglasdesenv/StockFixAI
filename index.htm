How It Works

1. The system audits and analyzes your sales data

StockFix connects to your ERP or POS and runs a full audit of your current inventory and past sales. It looks for inconsistencies, unexpected gaps, and patterns that could lead to stockouts or excess.

2. It identifies anomalies and critical products

Our AI pinpoints issues like missing SKUs, products with no recent movement, or items with negative inventory. It flags what’s urgent, what’s missing, and what’s being overstocked — with context.

3. It suggests inventory adjustments based on artificial intelligence

Every day, StockFix delivers clear, actionable suggestions to fix problems before they impact your revenue. It learns from your data and improves over time — helping you regain control of your inventory.
